Our Story

How It All Began

Screen VR Studios was founded by ScreenVR with a simple but powerful belief: to create VR experiences for the communtiy and to add to a new way to connect.

Starting of with just ScreenVR, dev-logs of Fear Massive were created, gaining interest from the community. Many people have helped test and suggest new ideas to the games created.

Today, we continue to grow - driven by the same excitement and curiosity that sparked it all.
